Frequently Asked Questions

How do AI credits work?

AI credits are consumed when you use our AI-powered features like content generation, campaign optimization, and market analysis. Different features consume different amounts of credits based on complexity.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll immediately gain access to additional features. When downgrading, the change will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a contract or commitment?

Our Basic and Professional plans are billed monthly with no long-term commitment required. Enterprise plans typically involve an annual contract, but we can customize terms based on your needs.

How does team member pricing work?

Pricing is per user, per month. Each team member gets their own login and personal workspace, while still being able to collaborate on shared projects.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or educational institutions?

Yes, we offer special pricing for qualified nonprofit organizations and educational institutions. Please contact our sales team to learn more.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and can arrange for invoice payment for Enterprise customers.

Need a custom solution?

Our Enterprise plan can be tailored to your organization's specific needs. Let's discuss how we can help you achieve your marketing goals.
